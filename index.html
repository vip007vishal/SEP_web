Executive Overview

Automating Complete Examination Lifecycles

Smart Exam Planner replaces tedious manual workflows with an integrated digital platform powered by modern allocation algorithms and Google Gemini AI.

"Educational institutions spend days organizing seating arrangements and resolving schedule conflicts. SEP reduces that workload to seconds."

Educational institutions face massive administrative overhead when preparing examination schedules, hall assignments, and invigilator rosters. These manual efforts consume days of labor and remain highly vulnerable to human error.

Strategic Pillars

Project Vision & Mission

Project Vision

The vision of Smart Exam Planner is to create a fully automated and intelligent examination management ecosystem that minimizes manual intervention, improves operational efficiency, and provides educational institutions with a secure, scalable, and future-ready solution for conducting examinations.

The long-term goal is to establish SEP as a cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform serving schools, colleges, universities, and examination authorities globally.

Project Mission

The mission of Smart Exam Planner is to simplify examination administration by replacing traditional manual processes with intelligent automation. The system aims to reduce planning time, eliminate scheduling conflicts, improve seating optimization, and provide administrators with complete control over examination operations.

By integrating Artificial Intelligence into examination planning, SEP ensures faster decision-making, greater accuracy, and improved resource utilization while maintaining institutional flexibility.

The Need for Change

Why Smart Exam Planner?

Most educational institutions still rely on spreadsheets, handwritten seating plans, and manually maintained examination records. As student counts increase, managing these resources becomes severely bottlenecked.

Spreadsheet & Manual Era

  • Days spent manually typing student records, hall numbers, and seating maps
  • High risk of double-assigned seats and hall overcrowding
  • High transcription error rates when copying exam timetables
  • Difficult to modify seating schedules after sudden weather or policy changes
  • Zero database security or multi-institution tenant isolation
  • Excessive stress for administrative staff and students during exam periods

Smart Exam Planner Era

  • Automatic seat allocation generated in under 10 seconds
  • Zero hall conflict guarantee through constraint-solving algorithms
  • AI Vision OCR automatically parses uploaded timetable images & PDFs
  • Dynamic rescheduling and instant PDF report regeneration
  • Multi-Tenant PostgreSQL database isolation for every institution
  • Centralized dashboard monitoring all exam sessions in real-time

The Modern Solution

Proposed System & AI Integration

Smart Exam Planner introduces a fully digital examination management platform automating the entire planning lifecycle.

Google Gemini AI Timetable Vision Processing

Instead of requiring manual data entry, administrators simply upload a timetable image, scanned notice, or PDF document directly into the dashboard.

Using the Google Gemini API, the system intelligently analyzes the uploaded document and extracts structured examination details—including subject names, subject codes, exam dates, FN/AN sessions, departments, semesters, and durations.

Once extracted, SEP automatically triggers its seating engine to generate non-conflicting arrangements based on hall capacities and institutional policies.

INPUT Upload Timetable Image / PDF
AI PARSE Google Gemini Vision OCR Engine
JSON PAYLOAD Subject Code, FN/AN Session, Exam Date
SOLVER Constraint-Based Seating Engine
OUTPUT PDF Seating Reports & Attendance Sheets

Architecture Breakdown

System Modules

Smart Exam Planner is designed as a modular ecosystem where each module executes specific responsibilities seamlessly.

M1

Institution Management

The foundation of the Multi-Tenant Architecture. Enables Super Admin to register institutions and provision dedicated PostgreSQL databases.

  • Institution Name, Code, Address & Connection String
  • Activation, Suspension & Status Controls
M2

User & Authentication

Enforces role-based access control (RBAC) protecting system features and tenant-isolated data operations.

  • Super Admin & Institution Admin privileges
  • JWT token authentication & RBAC protection
M3

Student Management

Central repository for thousands of student records with bulk Excel and CSV import capabilities.

  • Bulk Excel & CSV student ingestion
  • Register number, Department & Semester cataloging
M4

Examination Management

Defines exam schedules, maps subjects to departments, assigns FN/AN sessions, and tracks durations.

  • FN (Forenoon) / AN (Afternoon) session mapping
  • Exam rescheduling & cancellation tracking
M5

AI Vision Importer

Integrated OCR tool consuming timetable uploads via Google Gemini API to return structured JSON payloads.

  • Timetable image & PDF scanning
  • Auto-extracts subject codes, dates & sessions
M6

Hall Management

Stores physical classroom data: hall numbers, blocks, floors, row/column dimensions, and total capacities.

  • Block, floor & seat capacity tracking
  • Accessibility & hall availability statuses
M7

Seating Arrangement Engine

The core solver evaluating hall capacities, student numbers, branches, and exam sessions to yield non-conflicting seating.

  • Algorithmic seating optimization
  • Multi-constraint conflict resolution
M8

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time visual monitoring displaying student stats, hall utilization rates, exam progress, and system activity logs.

  • Live seat occupancy charts
  • Institution telemetry overview
M9

Report Generation Module

Produces professional, print-ready PDF reports including seating charts, hall lists, attendance rosters, and statistics.

  • PDF Seating Notices & Hall Lists
  • Attendance sheets & Invigilator duty rosters

Cloud Infrastructure

Multi-Tenant Architecture

SaaS Multi-Tenant Architecture

Smart Exam Planner follows a scalable Multi-Tenant Architecture designed for Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). Every registered educational institution operates on an isolated PostgreSQL database while sharing central application backend microservices.

Central Router & API Gateway

Handles JWT authentication, institution lookup & tenant request dispatching.

Master Database

Stores global tenant metadata, authentication credentials, and platform subscriptions.

Isolated Tenant DBs

Dedicated PostgreSQL DB for Institution 1, Institution 2, etc., storing students, halls & exams.
